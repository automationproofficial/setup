⚡ What Is Automation Pro Workflow?

Automation Pro is a plug-and-play automation system that clones social media videos and auto-posts them to your account — completely hands-free.

Built on top of the powerful open-source platform n8n that can run on the cloud or 100% locally on your machine using Docker.

✅ What It Does

🎬

Downloads Videos

From your Google Drive folder automatically

🚀

Auto Upload

📅

Scheduled Posts
